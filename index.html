Questions

Frequently Asked

Do I need to be home when you come?
Nope — as long as we have access to the backyard if needed, you don't need to be there. We'll send you a notification when the job is done.
What if it rains on my scheduled day?
We'll reschedule to the next available day at no extra charge. We'll always reach out in advance if weather is a factor.
Are there contracts or minimum commitments?
None whatsoever. You can book once, weekly, or biweekly — and cancel or adjust anytime. No lock-ins.
Do you bring your own equipment?
Yes — we arrive fully equipped with commercial-grade mowers, edgers, and trimmers. You don't need to provide anything.
What areas do you service?
We serve Mississauga, Brampton, Vaughan, Oakville, Richmond Hill, Etobicoke, and Markham. Not sure if we cover your area? Just call us.
