Questions

Frequently Asked

What's the best time of year to trim trees?
Late winter or early spring before new growth is ideal for most trees and shrubs. However, we can safely trim most species year-round — we'll advise you on timing for your specific plants.
Can you remove branches near my house or power lines?
Yes — we handle branches near structures. For branches near high-voltage power lines, we'll advise whether a certified arborist is required for safety.
Do you haul away the clippings?
Always. Full debris removal and cleanup is included in every job. We leave your yard tidy.
How often should I trim my hedges?
Most hedges benefit from 2–3 trims per season. Cedars typically need one main shaping per year. We'll tell you what suits your specific shrubs.
Will trimming hurt my trees?
Done correctly, pruning promotes healthier growth. We only remove what's necessary and make clean cuts that heal properly.

Questions

Frequently Asked

When should I book a spring cleanup?
Late March through April is ideal for spring cleanup — once the snow is gone and ground is dry enough to walk on. Book early, we fill up quickly.
When should I book a fall cleanup?
Mid-October through November works best. We aim to get leaves after the majority have fallen but before the ground freezes.
Do you take the leaves away?
Yes — all debris including leaves, clippings, and organic material is hauled off your property. No piles left behind.
Is aeration really necessary every year?
For most Ontario lawns, annual aeration makes a big difference — especially on clay-heavy soils common in the GTA. It opens up the root zone and dramatically improves lawn health.
Can I book spring and fall cleanups together?
Absolutely — and many customers do. It's the easiest way to make sure your yard is prepped properly for both the growing season and winter.

Questions

Frequently Asked

How long does a full transformation take?
Most full-property projects run 3–7 days depending on scope. We'll give you a clear schedule during the proposal stage.
Do I need to move out or be away during the project?
Not at all — we work cleanly and professionally. You're welcome to be home or away. We'll coordinate access as needed.
Can we do the project in phases?
Absolutely. Many homeowners prefer to phase the work over one or two seasons. We design the full plan upfront so every phase fits together perfectly.
Will you work with my existing features?
Yes — we assess what's worth keeping, improving, or replacing. Your existing trees, structures, and hardscape are all factored into the design.
How do I know what things will look like?
We walk you through the full plan before anything begins. You'll know exactly what to expect in every area of the yard.
